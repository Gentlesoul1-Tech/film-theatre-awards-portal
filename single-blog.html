Biennale Teatro 2025

Theme: Theatre is Body – Body is Poetry

Dates: May 31 – June 15, 2025

Director: Willem Dafoe

Highlights

  • Venice 75/25: Celebrating 50 years of new theatre with performances by Odin Teatret and The Wooster Group.
  • Contemporary Masters: Works by Romeo Castellucci, Thomas Ostermeier, and Milo Rau.
  • Emerging Voices: Premieres from Evangelia Randou, Princess Bangura, and Anthony Nikolchev.
  • Biennale College Teatro: Showcasing new talents and offering workshops by industry leaders.

Attendance

Venues: Various locations across Venice, including Teatro alle Tese and Isola del Lazzaretto Vecchio.

Tickets: Purchase online or at venue box offices one hour before performances.

Nigerian Filmmakers Pivot to YouTube

Streaming platforms retreat, creators find new success online

In March 2025, Omoni Oboli's romantic comedy Love in Every Word captivated Nigerian audiences, amassing over 5 million YouTube views within three days. As global streaming giants like Amazon Prime and Netflix scale back African operations, Nigerian filmmakers are increasingly turning to YouTube to reach audiences directly.

While YouTube offers creative freedom and broad reach, challenges such as piracy and limited monetization persist. Nonetheless, filmmakers like Oboli are embracing the platform to connect with viewers and sustain their craft.
