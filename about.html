JOS INTERNATIONAL
THEATRE AND FILM FESTIVAL (JITAFF)

The Jos International Theatre And Film Festival is an initiative of a group of Ex-students of the Theatre Arts Department of the Plateau State University and the University of Jos Communications, Theatre and Film Department in Nigeria paying tribute to these two monumental institutions that have produced practitioners that are making tremendous impact in the arts internationally. JITAFF is therefore born out of respect for professional theatre and film foundations. Broadly speaking, JITAFF receives entries in Theatre in video and Film. We proudly celebrate the innovative genre of Theatre in video. After the theatre experience the audience go back home with different levels of catharsis. Now we give our audience the opportunity to enjoy professional theatre on their hand gadgets and big screen screens anytime. We celebrate the creativity of film and Theatre producers from all over the world. Cultural representation is a major component of Theatre and Film. JITAFF celebrates creative cultural works from all around the globe. Making a film or theatrical work is not easy from any angle. Bring your work, the product of your sweat to Jos. We will celebrate with your while you share ideas and experiences with other filmmakers from all over the world.
