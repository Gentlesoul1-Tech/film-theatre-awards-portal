JOS Festival Submission Portal

Submission Conditions

  • Submissions must be original works by the applicant.
  • Only films and theatre performances completed after January 1, 2023, are eligible.
  • Entries must be in English or have English subtitles.
  • Accepted formats: MP4, MOV, AVI, or MKV for films; stage recordings for theatre performances.
  • Maximum duration: 30 minutes for short films; 60 minutes for theatre performances.
  • Each submission must include a title, brief synopsis, and filmmaker/performer bio.
  • One submission per applicant is allowed.
  • Deadline for submission is September 30, 2025.
  • Submission does not guarantee selection. A jury will screen and shortlist entries.
  • No entry fee is required for the 2025 edition.

By submitting, you agree to allow JOS Festival to use your work for promotional and exhibition purposes.
